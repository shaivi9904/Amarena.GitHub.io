Loco for Cocoa

By Shaivi Shah on August 2, 2017

This creamy and rich ice cream has four ingredients which includes the velvety chocolate. This is a No-machine ice cream so it doesn't use an ice cream machine.After tasting this charm you will never buy store-bought chocolate ice cream ever again!

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup of Chocolate
  2. 1 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream
  3. 1/2 cup of condensed milk
  4. 1 tspn of vanilla extract

Instructions:

First, take your heavy whipping cream and whipp it until soft peaks form. Then, add your condensed milk and whipp until well combined. Put your vanilla extract into the mixture and mix well. Take your semi sweet or milk chocolate and melt in the microwave and mix it well into your ice cream base. Let your ice cream freeze 6-8 hours preferbly overnight. To prevent ice crystals from forming, every 45 minutes take your ice cream out of the freezer and whisk for the next 6-8 hours. I hope you liked this recipe! back to recipes
