Vanilla Ice Cream

By Shaivi Shah on August 2, 2017

This delicious ice cream is simple but becomes everyones favorite. It uses fresh vanilla bean which will make your ice cream taste 10 times better. As usual this recipe will take 6-8 hours to freeze preferably overnight. Also, this makes about 5 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 1 1/2 cup of whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of condensed milk
  • 1 tspn of vanilla extract

Instructions:

Take your heavy whipping cream and place in a large bowl then whipp it until soft peaks form. Once your heavy cream has formed soft peaks, add your condensed milk and whipp until it has been well combined. Finally add your vanilla extract. For the intense vanilla flavor, cut open your vanilla pod (vanilla bean) in half. Scrape out all the seeds from the inside and add it into your ice cream base. Let your ice cream freeze for about 6-8 hours preferably overnight. To prevent any ice crystals from forming every 45 minutes take your mixture out and give a quick whisk. Enjoy!
