No Machine-Strawberry Ice Cream

By Shaivi Shah on August 2, 2017

This true delight is made with three simple ingredients and the best part about is that it uses fresh strawberry. This recipe doesn't use an ice cream machine and it needs 6-8 hours to freeze but overnight is better. This recipe calls for about 5 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of strawberries
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of condensed milk
  • 1 tspn of vanilla extract

Instructions:

Take your heavy whipping cream and place in a large bowl then whipp it until soft peaks form. Once your heavy cream has formed soft peaks, add your condensed milk and whipp until it has been well combined. Finally add your vanilla extract. To make the strawberry flavor, puree back to recipes
